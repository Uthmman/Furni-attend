# **App Name**: FurnishWise

## Core Features:

- Order Management: Create and manage furniture orders including order description, product picture attachment, customer name, order date, and order status.
- Item Usage Tracking: Display items used in the order, stock decreases after verification.
- Store Management: Add, update, and manage furniture items in the store, including unit of measurement and stock levels.
- Stock Adjustment: Enable manual adjustment of stock levels with an intuitive UI, including a log of changes. Button to add, decrease, or use an item's stock.
- Employee Attendance: Record employee attendance with morning and afternoon entry times, tracking absent, present, and late statuses.
- Payroll Calculator: Calculate payments based on attendance records, with weekly payments calculated from daily rates and monthly payments from monthly rates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#228B22) evoking nature and craftsmanship.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC), creating a warm and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Warm mustard (#FFDB58) highlights important actions and information.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' (serif) for headlines, imparting a high-end feel, paired with 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text, to ensure readability.
- Use minimalist, line-style icons to represent furniture, attendance, and management functions.
- Clean and structured layout with clear sections for order details, item management, and employee information.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback, providing a polished user experience.
